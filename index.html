A few snippets of our innovation journey



Vulcan Platform

Allowlist management Platform

Vulcan platform that allows NEAR NFT/DeFi projects to manage their Allowlist easily. The platform users can claim and manage all project Whitelists in one place. It is used by 5000+ unique wallets and has served 25+ projects.

Amplify Platform - ICP

Social-Fi Application

Amplify, a Social-Fi platform, enables projects and thought leaders to boost engagement on their social media posts by rewarding interacting users. Version 1.0 currently enhances Twitter posts, with plans to expand into additional Web2 and Web3 social media applications. It is being built with support from DFinity Foundation (ICP Blockchain).

Unison Platform

Collab Automation platform

Unison Collab Automation Platform is an attempt to save project time and resources by creating an easy-to-use dashboard and a Discord Bot for NFT projects to request/accept collaborations, start raffles, and auto-assign Allowlist roles to winners of raffles. Besides automating collaborations, Unison aids collab managers in finding new projects in a unified dashboard.
