Last Updated: 11th June, 2025

At NearverseLabs, we take pride in offering digital sports collectibles that bring joy and utility to our community. Please read our refund policy carefully before making a purchase.

All sales of digital collectibles on our platform are final and non-refundable. Since these are digital items delivered immediately upon purchase, we cannot issue refunds once the transaction is completed.

We will review refund requests under the following exceptional cases:

If any of these apply, please contact us within 7 days of the transaction.

3. How to Request a Refund

Email us at team@nearverselabs.com with your order ID, transaction details, and a brief description of the issue.

4. Response Timeline

We aim to resolve refund requests within 7 business days of receiving complete information.
